Twelve annotation challenge types

Every order maps to one challenge type, tiered by complexity. New miners start on Tier 1 and unlock higher tiers as trust compounds.

01Tier 1

Episode-level annotation

High-level labels per trajectory: instruction, objects, outcome.

02Tier 2

Action labelling

Timestamped atomic skills: reach, grasp, transport, place, release.

03Tier 1

Clipping

Cut raw recordings into clean units, stripping idle and reset frames.

04Tier 3

Hand pose estimation

Per-frame 3D hand and finger keypoints, correcting an estimator.

05Tier 2

6-DoF object pose

Position and orientation tracks, consistent through occlusion.

06Tier 2

07Tier 3

Affordance & grasp

Graspable regions and contact points, validated for reachability.

08Tier 3
