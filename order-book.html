Challenge catalog

Twelve annotation challenge types

Every order maps to one challenge type, tiered by complexity. New miners start on Tier 1 and unlock higher tiers as trust compounds.

01Tier 1

Episode-level annotation

One or a few high-level labels per trajectory: the instruction, objects, and outcome. Grounds language to behaviour.

Task: Write 5–10 instructions describing this episode.
02Tier 2

Action labelling

Decompose the episode into timestamped atomic skills. Unlocks long-horizon tasks and skill recombination.

Task: Mark the start and end frame of each primitive.
03Tier 1

Clipping

Cut raw recordings into clean units and strip idle and reset frames that poison behavioural cloning.

Task: Split a 10-minute session into pick-place episodes.
04Tier 3

Hand pose estimation

Per-frame 3D hand and finger keypoints, correcting an automated estimator. The bridge from human video to gripper.

Task: Correct the 21 keypoints where the estimator drifted.
05Tier 2

6-DoF object pose

Full position and orientation tracks for objects and end-effectors, consistent through occlusion, against RGB-D.

Task: Annotate the mug's 6-DoF pose across all visible frames.
06Tier 2

Point-cloud labelling

Semantic and instance segmentation of fused LiDAR and multi-camera scenes, with persistent track IDs.

Task: Segment every graspable instance and assign a track ID.
07Tier 3

Affordance & grasp

Graspable regions and functional contact points, validated for reachability and collision against gripper geometry.

Task: Mark valid grasp points on the drill and its grip axis.
08Tier 3

Force / tactile events

Mark contact onset, slip, and insertion on synchronized force-torque and tactile streams. Scarce and high-value.

Task: Mark first contact and successful peg insertion frames.
09Tier 1

Language instructions

Write and diversify natural-language instructions at consistent granularity for instruction-conditioned VLAs.

Task: Paraphrase into two alternatives preserving the goal.
10Tier 2

Reward & progress

Per-frame progress scores and sub-goal markers, providing dense supervision for reward models and value functions.

Task: Assign a monotonic 0-to-1 progress score per frame.
11Tier 1

Success / failure & safety

Classify outcomes and flag collisions, drops, and near-misses with timestamps. Foundational for filtering and safety.

Task: Flag every arm-environment collision with timestamps.
12Tier 2

Multi-view sync & calibration

Verify cross-camera time alignment and extrinsic calibration so every stream shares a drift-free timeline.

Task: Flag any drift across the four views and proprioception.

Summary

Stereo RGB + IMU egocentric capture requiring 21-joint hand keypoint correction and per-frame contact state.

Capture workflow

  1. Receive the raw demonstration pack and the matching stereo calibration file.
  2. Align each clip to the supplied world coordinate frame in your tool of choice.
  3. Annotate against the rubric, then run the local schema validator before you submit.

Acceptance rubric

  • All keypoints present on every frame the hand is visible.
  • Contact-state transitions correct to within two frames of true contact.
  • Submission passes the geometric consistency check against the calibration file.
  • Hidden honeypot clips scored at or above the order's acceptance threshold.

Capture specification

  • Embodiment: egocentric human. Sensors: stereo RGB + IMU at 30 fps.
  • Episode length 8–40 seconds. Required modalities: video, hand trajectory, contact.
